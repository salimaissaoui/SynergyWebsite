Brief Introduction to PFAS

Per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances (PFAS) are a class of synthetic chemicals widely used in industrial applications due to their resistance to water, grease, and heat. Among the most well-known PFAS compounds are Perfluorooctane Sulfonate (PFOS) and Perfluorooctanoic Acid (PFOA).

PFOS: Used in firefighting foams and water-resistant coatings

PFOA: Key component in non-stick cookware like Teflon

There are more than 12,000 different variants of PFAS.
